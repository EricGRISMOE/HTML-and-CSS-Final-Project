Directions For The Perfect Burger

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Total Time: 45 mins

Directions

  1. One top and bottom 9-grain bun
  2. Butter the insides of both peices and heat on flat-top grill for 3 1/2 min.
  3. Dice 1/8 inch slices of tomato, onion and lettuce
  4. Grab desired brand of patty and grill for 15 mins under cover or at least untill very little pink inside
  5. Remove bun from grill when golden on the inside and put a layer of desired condiment
  6. NEXT PART VERY IMPORTANT
  7. In order:
  8. Bottom Bun
  9. Patty
  10. If wanted put slight slat and or pepper here
  11. Tomato
  12. Onion
  13. Lettuce
  14. Shich kebab stick directly through the top center


  • Once finished enjoy the hardwork you put in
